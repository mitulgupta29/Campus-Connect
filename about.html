About Campus Connect

Campus Connect is a web-based platform built by IPM students at IIM Rohtak to streamline academic collaboration, campus event engagement, and student networking.

This project was developed as part of the Web-Based Development course using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It is designed to be a centralized hub for students to access study materials, stay informed about events, and connect with peers.
